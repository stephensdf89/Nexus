Data Deletion Instructions

How to request the removal of your personal data from Content Creator Nexus

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

If you have used Facebook Login to connect your account to Content Creator Nexus, you may request the deletion of your data at any time. We are committed to honoring all data deletion requests promptly and transparently.

What Data We Delete

When a deletion request is processed, we remove the following:

  • Your Content Creator Nexus account and profile information
  • Any content you uploaded or created on the platform
  • Your activity history, messages, and preferences
  • Your connection to any social accounts (including Facebook/Meta)
  • Any derived data and analytics tied to your user ID

Note: Some data may be retained where required by law (e.g., financial transaction records for tax/legal compliance, for up to 7 years). Aggregate, anonymized data from which you cannot be identified may be retained.

How to Request Deletion

Method 1

Delete via Account Settings

Fastest option

  1. 1Log in to your Content Creator Nexus account
  2. 2Go to Account Settings (top right menu)
  3. 3Click "Privacy & Data"
  4. 4Click "Delete My Account & Data"
  5. 5Confirm deletion — you will receive a confirmation email
Method 2

Submit a Deletion Request by Email

For users who prefer email communication

  1. 1Compose an email to privacy@contentcreatornexus.com
  2. 2Use subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. 3Include: your registered email address and/or username, and a note that you wish to delete all your data
  4. 4We will respond within 5 business days to confirm receipt
  5. 5Deletion will be completed within 30 days
Method 3

Facebook / Meta Users

If you used Facebook Login and no longer have access to your account

  1. 1Email privacy@contentcreatornexus.com
  2. 2Subject: "Facebook Data Deletion Request"
  3. 3Include your Facebook name and the email associated with your Meta account
  4. 4We will locate and delete all data tied to that Facebook login

What Happens After You Request Deletion

You will receive a confirmation email within 5 business days acknowledging your request.

Your data will be fully deleted within 30 days of confirmation.

You will receive a final confirmation email when deletion is complete.

Your account will be immediately deactivated upon request; login will be disabled.

Any active subscriptions or partnerships will be terminated.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Confirmation & Tracking

Once your deletion request is received, we will send you a confirmation email with a unique request ID. You can reference this ID when following up. If you do not receive a confirmation within 5 business days, please check your spam folder or re-submit your request.
