1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, profile photo, and other registration details you provide.
  • Profile & Content Data: Creator portfolio content, bio, social media handles, niche categories, collaboration history, and any content you upload or publish on the platform.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns, search queries within the platform, and interaction logs.
  • Device & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and referring URLs.
  • Communications: Messages sent through our platform, support tickets, and feedback you submit.
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through our third-party payment processors (we do not store full card numbers).
  • Social Media Data: If you connect social accounts (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, etc.), we may receive profile data and analytics permitted by those platforms.
  • Cookies & Tracking: See Section 5 (Cookies & Tracking Technologies) for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Content Creator Nexus platform and services
  • Personalize your experience and surface relevant brand partnerships and collaborators
  • Process transactions and send related receipts and notifications
  • Send platform updates, newsletters, and promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Analyze usage trends to improve features and fix bugs
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Generate anonymized, aggregated analytics for internal and business purposes

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties that help operate the platform (hosting, payment processing, email delivery, analytics, customer support). They are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Brand Partners: If you opt into brand partnership matching, your creator profile (public portions) is shared with interested brands. You control what is public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights, safety, or property of Content Creator Nexus, our users, or the public.
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing will only occur with your explicit consent.
  • Aggregated/De-identified Data: We may share non-identifying, aggregated statistics publicly or with partners.

4. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (see Section 9 for our Data Deletion process).
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link or your account settings.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
  • Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • CCPA Rights (California residents): Right to know, right to delete, right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell data), and right to non-discrimination.
  • GDPR Rights (EU/EEA residents): All rights listed above plus the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@contentcreatornexus.com.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in and remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage via analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Deliver relevant features and personalize your experience
  • Measure the effectiveness of campaigns

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and choices.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant promotions (with consent).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality. We honor "Do Not Track" signals where technically feasible.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account, plus 30 days for recovery.
  • Usage logs: Retained for up to 24 months for analytics and security purposes.
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years to comply with financial regulations.
  • Support communications: Retained for 3 years.
  • Backup copies: May persist for up to 90 days after deletion requests are processed.

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive stored data
  • Access controls and role-based permissions for our team
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Incident response procedures for potential data breaches
  • Two-factor authentication options for user accounts

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

8. Children's Privacy

Content Creator Nexus is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are between 13 and 17, you may use the platform only with verified parental or guardian consent.

If we discover we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us at privacy@contentcreatornexus.com.

9. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. Here's how:

  • In-App: Go to Account Settings → Privacy → Delete My Data
  • By Email: Send a deletion request to privacy@contentcreatornexus.com with subject line "Data Deletion Request" and your registered email address.
  • Full Account Deletion: Go to Account Settings → Delete Account — this permanently removes your profile, content, and associated data.

We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Some data may be retained longer where required by law (e.g., financial records). We will confirm completion of your request via email.

For Facebook/Meta Login users, you may also submit a deletion request via our Data Deletion page at /data-deletion.

10. Third-Party Services & Links

Our platform integrates with or links to third-party services including social media platforms, payment processors, and analytics tools. This Privacy Policy does not govern third-party data practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party service you use in connection with Content Creator Nexus.

Third-party integrations we use may include: Meta/Facebook, Google, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Stripe, and others. Each has its own privacy practices.

11. International Data Transfers

Content Creator Nexus is based in Oklahoma, United States. If you access our platform from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. or other countries. By using the platform, you consent to this transfer.

For users in the EU/EEA, we implement appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required by GDPR.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or a banner on the platform at least 14 days before changes take effect.

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the platform after the effective date constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions, concerns, or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@contentcreatornexus.com
  • Subject line for requests: "Privacy Request – [Your Name]"
  • Mailing Address: Content Creator Nexus Privacy Team, Oklahoma, United States
  • Response time: Within 5–7 business days; deletion requests within 30 days
